About Abhishek

I started
late.
So did you.

Start your journey

I'm a software developer and distance runner from Hyderabad. I picked up chess at 42 — no background, no coach, no clue what the Sicilian Defense was. Just curiosity and a willingness to lose a lot of games.

"The best time to start was when you were a kid. The second best time is right now."

I document every game on YouTube — @indianthinkingathlete. Every blunder, every good move, every game where I got checkmated in 12 moves. The journey is the content.

That honesty is what makes me a better coach. I know what it feels like to not understand why you keep losing. I know the frustration of hanging pieces you didn't even see. I teach the way I wish someone had taught me — clear, direct, and without the jargon.
